Research

My research focuses on systems and control theory and its applications. I am currently working on direct data-driven methods for control of linear and nonlinear systems. This concerns the cases where partial or no prior model knowledge of the system is available and instead one has access to (limited) input-output data generated from the system. Previously, I worked on modeling the blood pressure response to fluid infusion and hemorrhage then designed and tested (in-silico) an adaptive controller for closed-loop fluid resuscitation.
