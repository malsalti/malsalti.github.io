About

I am a research assistant at the Institute of Automatic Control at Leibniz University Hannover. My research focuses on the analysis and control of dynamical systems using data-driven and learning-based methods, with particular interest in nonlinear systems and biomedical applications.

Research interests

My research lies at the intersection of control theory, dynamical systems, and machine learning.

  • Analysis and control of nonlinear systems
  • Data- and learning-based analysis and control
  • Biomedical applications
  • Intersection of machine learning and control theory
